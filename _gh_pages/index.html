Why Choose AP&G?

Discover the benefits of switching to AP&G for your energy needs

Competitive Rates

We offer some of the most flexible and competitive energy rates in the market, helping you save money on your monthly bills.

Flexible Plans

Choose the plan that best fits your needs, whether you're looking for fixed rates, variable rates, or green energy options.

Exceptional Service

AP&G prioritizes customer satisfaction with dedicated support from our U.S. based customer support team members.

Sustainable Choices

We’re committed to offering certified renewable energy from hydro, solar, and wind sources to help reduce your carbon footprint with every KWh you use.

Seamless Transition

Switching to AP&G is fast, easy and requires no special visits or extra steps. We will inform your old supplier and ensure there are no disruptions in your service.

Superior Benefits

AP&G goes above and beyond for our customers, including benefits like: complimentary home warranty services, premier rewards, and our yearly 25% rebate check.

Partnering with Your Utility

We work with your utility, not replace them.

While utilities maintain the wires and pipelines to ensure reliable electricity and gas, we provide clean energy from our wind or solar farms at competitive prices. Together, we deliver better energy solutions.
